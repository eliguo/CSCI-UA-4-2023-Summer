About Blue Bloods

Our Mission

For centuries, horseshoe crabs have silently meandered our ocean floors, playing a vital role in the marine ecosystem and serving as a window into Earth's evolutionary past. Blue Bloods is dedicated to the protection, conservation, and advocacy of these ancient mariners. We believe that by understanding and championing the cause of the horseshoe crabs, we not only preserve a critical piece of marine biodiversity but also protect the very essence of life as we know it.

Why Horseshoe Crabs?

Horseshoe crabs are not just ancient relics of the past; they are modern-day heroes in biomedical research. Their blue blood contains a unique substance, Limulus Amebocyte Lysate (LAL), crucial for testing the sterility of medical equipment and vaccines, ensuring human health and safety.

Yet, despite their significance, horseshoe crab populations face threats from habitat loss, overharvesting, and climate change. It's a race against time to save these marvelous creatures from decline.
